Welcome to the Aurora images page!

Aurora lights, also known as the Northern or Southern Lights (Aurora Borealis and Aurora Australis), are natural light displays in the sky caused by charged particles from the sun colliding with Earth's atmosphere. These collisions excite atmospheric gases, creating shimmering colors like green, pink, purple, and red. They are most commonly seen near polar regions and have fascinated humans for centuries with their magical, dancing patterns.
